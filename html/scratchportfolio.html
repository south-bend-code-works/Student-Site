Example:
iframe tag

Use an <iframe> tag to add your Scratch project to your website.

In your HTML type opening and closing <iframe>tags.

<iframe> </iframe>

In between the iframe tags, type src=" "

In between the quotes, paste the link to your Scratch project.
Example:

<iframe src=" https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/290070387/ "></iframe>

To add a window to your game, add the word "embed" to your source code.

<iframe src="https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/embed/290070387/"></iframe>

Add width and height.

<iframe width="700" height="400"src="https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/embed/290070387/"></iframe>

Change the width and height to the dimsensions that you like.

All together, your code should look like this:

<iframe width="700" height="400" src="https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/embed/217655836"> </iframe>

Add more of your Scratch projects to your website.
