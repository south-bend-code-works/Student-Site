Brainstorm

Pick a theme for your game.
Examples of themes:
  1. fantasy
  2. mystery
  3. survival
  4. love
  5. adventure

Answer these questions about your game:

Who is your main character?
  1. What can your main character do? Can he or she fly, swim, and teleport?
  2. What is your main character's personality?
  3. What types of decisions will your user be faced with?
  4. Are there enemies?
  5. Can the player get rejuvenated?
Design of Your Game
  1. Is there music playing in the background when a person plays your game?
    Rock, rap, techno?
  2. What types of images can bring your game to life?

1st Game Screen
This is the first screen that your user sees after he/she clicked "Play".

For example:

Create a new HTML file.

Name the file "screen1.html"

Be sure to save this file in your "YourFirstName_ChooseYourOwnAdventure" folder.

Add text and two choices for your user.

For example:

When you're ready to start styling the page, be sure to link your CSS file ("style.css") to your new HTML file.
Links

Link your Welcome Screen + 1st Game Screen

Find your Play button in your code.

<button>Play!</button>

Use the <a> tag to link your button.

<a href = "screen1.html"><button>Play!</button></a>

Code the rest of the game's pages.
