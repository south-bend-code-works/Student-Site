Career Test

What is a career test?

Career tests are tools that help you understand how a wide range of characteristics impact your career success and enjoyment. A test like this is designed to provide customized career options that are suited for your workstyle.


What does this mean?

People work differently. A test like this is designed to help you learn what kind of work enviroment is best suited for you.

While career tests are not the golden rule, it is a great way to start to understand how you learn and work.


But how does a personality type connect to the career of my dreams?

If you are quiet and prefer to work alone, you can have a different career than someone who likes to work in a team where there is constant interaction and feedback.

Different skillsets work better in different careers, and this is great tool to figure out what those are!
