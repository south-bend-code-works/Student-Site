Python Turtle Basics

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create a special Repl environment to work with Turtles!
  2. Draw a picture using a turtle.
Background Info

What is a turtle?

A turtle in Python is kind of like an Etch-a-Sketch, it's a drawing board which lets us command a turtle to draw.

Drawing a picture

Get out a piece of paper and a pencil or pen.

Draw a simple picture using straight lines, without picking up your pencil or pen off the paper.
Start in the middle of your paper.

Now let's recreate your paper drawing with the turtle.

What direction does your first line go in?
Tell your turtle to turn to match that direction.

How long is your first line?
Tell the turtle to move forward that far.

What direction is the next line?
Tell the turtle to turn that way, then proceed ahead!

You might need to tell the turtle to go backward sometimes.

Keep drawing, following the steps you took on your paper drawing.
Add code until you've recreated your picture!

Show an instructor your completed picture!
No shaking required!
