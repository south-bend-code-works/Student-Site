We can customize our turtles and make them draw with different colors and shapes.
Letâ€™s start with drawing circles. Use the circle() method to draw a circle. Circle needs a radius.

We can also draw partial circles using two other parameters.

Circle(radius, extent, steps). Radius tells how big the circle will be. Extent tells how far around the circle will go (360 is a complete circle). Steps determines how many sides the circle shape will have (use 3 for a triangle and 4 for a square).

You can also draw partial circles by using a smaller extent.

Colors

We can also fill our shapes with different colors.

Try t.fillcolor(color) to set the fillcolor.

Then use t.begin_fill() before your shape, and t.end_fill() after your shape.

Use shape fill to add to your picture.
