Color Theory

Understanding how color is formed and the relationships between different colors, can help you to use color more effectively in your designs, and help you pick the right color palette for your projects.


A Brief Lesson on Color Theory from Rhea Lelina Manglapus on Vimeo.

A Few Definitions

Hue
Hue is in degrees (around the color wheel), so a yellow color will appear between 50 and 60 degrees, with the perfect yellow appearing at 56 degrees. Green, meanwhile, appears at 120 degrees on the wheel at so on.
Saturation
Saturation is a % for how saturated (or rich) a color is. Low saturation means there is less overall color, eventually becoming a shade of grey when fully desaturated.
Brightness
Brightness is how bright a color is,between 0% - 100%. A yellow at 0% brightness will be black, while the same yellow hue and saturation at 100% brightness will be the full yellow color.
