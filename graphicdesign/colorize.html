Colorize

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create a colorized black & white photo

Step #1 - Plan

Find a black and white image that you want to restore color to.

To find an image, you can use Canva or use another image.
Step #2 - Open GIMP

Open the image that you're using in GIMP.

On the right-hand side, click to make sure that you have an Alpha Channel Added. If that option is available, click "Add Alpha Channel" (like you did in the Geometric poster exercise).

Hint:
Step #3 - Add Color

Go through and add color to each segment of your image.

To get started, follow along with the tutorial video below:

Follow the same steps to add color to every segment of your image.

Go into as much detail as you would like. When you finish, save your project.
