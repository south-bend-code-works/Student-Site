Challenge #1 - Design Your Own Logo
In this lesson you will:
  1. Learn about the importance of logos.
  2. Create your own logo
Background Info

A logo is a a symbol or other design adopted by an organization to identify its products, uniform, vehicles, etc.

Logos are important because they are the first design that a client sees.

Get Started!

Step #1-Plan

The 1st step of Graphic Design is to Research & Plan. Since this project is about you, we're going to do a little bit of research about you by asking you a few questions!

Take 30 sec to answer the following questions. Start the timer when you're ready!

Step #2-Mockup

A mockup is a prototype that allows a designer to test designs.

Brainstorm and sketch different logo examples.

Ask an instructor for a piece of paper and pen or pencil.

Draw a few quarter sized sketches of different logo styles for your personal logo. Try to integrate some of the things that you said about yourself.
Step #3-Build

Create a digital version of your logo by making your own computer graphic.

To get started, open GIMP, the creative tool that we'll be using. Find GIMP on your computer's desktop.

Once you've opened GIMP, split your screen so that GIMP is on one half of your screen and this tutorial video is on the other half.

Click here to open the tutorial video: https://www.youtube.com/watch

First, practice making shapes and filling them in using GIMP. This will help you to get started with making your first logo!
Step #4-Review

Take a look at your logo! Are there any changes that you want to make?

If there are any changes that you want to make, revise your logo now.
