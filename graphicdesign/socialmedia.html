Design a social media ad

In this lesson you will:
  1. Learn about social media
  2. Create your own social media ad

Social media is a viable tool for companies to interact directly with their current and future users.

Example ads:

Now it's time to apply the lessons that you learned about branding to design a social media ad for a client.
Here's your Challenge:

Your client is looking for social media ads to attract new clients to his new bakery. He wants to focus on his food being healthy, fresh, and delicious. Look at the above examples and design four ad options for the baker.

Step #1 - Plan

Think about the mood that your ads should convey:

- Should they be feminine, cheerful, serious, or elegant?
- Who is the baker's target audience?
- What message does the baker want to convey?
Step #2 - Mockup

Sketch examples of your ads.
Step #3 - Build

Log into your Canva account.

Use stock photos and make social media posts to advertise for your client.

Step #4 - Design Review + Critique

The critique is a fundamental part of the design process. This helps you discuss ideas out with your peers and instructors. Be prepared for criticism.

It's time to get feedback!

Peer Feedback

Choose two peers. Ask them for honest feedback on your ads. Ask them what they like. What they don't like. Take notes, and ask what they would change.
Instructor Feedback

Ask one of your instructors to critique your design. Set up a time to meet and get back together to chat about your project. Ask them what they like. What they don't like. Take notes, and ask what they would change.
