Behance Portfolio

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create a design portfolio
Part of being a designer is having a portfolio!
You've created multiple great projects that you can add to your portfolio.
It's time to create a portfolio in Behance, an online platform for showcasing & discovering creative work!
Step #1 - Register with Behance

Set up an account with Behance.
Step #2 - Upload your first project

Clearly marked buttons make it easy to get started!

Once you upload your images, Behance lets you add text and links and personalise your page.

It's very easy to select and edit colors, typefaces, text, etc. using the built-in panel.

Remember, this portfolio is made to showcase your design skills so take care in making your Behance page look good!
Step #3 - Choose tags

Employers and creatives use tags when searching for work on Behance!

Using the right tags to describe your work is very important.
Step #4 - Upload all of your design projects

Add all of your best work to your Behance portfolio!
