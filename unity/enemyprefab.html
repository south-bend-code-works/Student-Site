Making a prefab

Drag the enemy from the Hierarchy view into your Prefab folder in the Project view. (Select Original Prefab).

Go ahead and position new enemies around your map by dragging the prefab out onto the scene.

What about having some enemies guard some of your collectables? Position new collectables and new enemies where you would like them.

Making New Kinds of Enemies

To add different kinds of enemies, you just need to get a new model! Go find one in the Asset Store.

Asset Store → 3D Models → Characters → Humanoids

Once you find a model, you can get it ready to replace the original enemy model. First, drag one of your enemy prefabs into the scene.

To swap out the model:

  1. Delete the old model. It is a child of the controller.
  2. Put the new model in.
  3. Replace the avatar.

Things you can edit on different enemies:

AttackDistance - some may be more aggressive than others

AttackDamage - some may deal more damage than others

Move Speed Multiplier - some may be faster!

Weapons

Try editing other values!

Once you have the enemy working how you want it, you can make a new prefab of it (you could have a prefab for each type of enemy)!
