Unity Cheat Sheet - Interface Basics

Forgot stuff? It happens!
Navigating the Scene View

To pan the view: hold down the middle button (scroll wheel)

To rotate the view: hold ALT and hold the left mouse button

To zoom: scroll wheel



Parent/Child Relationships

Lists all objects in the scene. Click arrows next to an object to see its children. Click Create to make new GameObjects.

When an object is nested under another, it's known as a child.



Holds all assets in your game. All assets are in subfolders under the big Assets folder. Can zoom in and out on the bottom right.



Shows all the components on the selected GameObject. Can adjust:


Name - Edit the box at the top.

Tag - Click the box next to the word Tag

Active State - Whether the object appears in the game. Click the checkbox next to the name.


Components:

  • Transform - This is where it is in the Scene view. You can adjust the position, rotation, and scale here. All GameObjects have a transform component.
  • Collider - The Hitbox of the object. This detects whether it touches other objects.

  • Scripts - Custom components. Public variables in scripts can be adjusted in here.
  • Different types of GameObjects have different unique components (Camera, Lights, etc.)
