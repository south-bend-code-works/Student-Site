Passion Project

Code a project about a topic that you're passionate about!

Example Projects:

Take a look at the projects listed below. If any projects are interesting to you, remix the project idea + make it your own! If you come up with a different project idea that isn't listed below, build it!
Remember, code always wins!

Cycology

This website was made by a group of students who were passionate about recycling. They built this site to help schools find crafts that they can make to practice recycling. Their goal was to make recycling fun.

Food4You

Food4You uses an algorithm to recommend restaurants based on the user's food cravings.

Art Share

Art Share was made by students to showcase local, high school students' art work, poems, and short stories. The coders behind this project want to highlight the importance of keeping fine arts programs in schools.

EatLocal

EatLocal recommends local restaurants to help promote local businesses.

Water Tracker

Water Tracker helps users to monitor their daily water in take. The app also includes "fun fact" reminders of the importance of staying hydrated!

Good Samaritan

Good Samaritan provides users with helpful info in case they encounter small emergencies.

Falcam

Our students made Falcam for the City of South Bend. This website includes a link to the city's live feed of the city's falcon nest. The live feed has currently been taken down by the City of South Bend; however, the website is still up in case the falcons return! The site received thousands of views from users who were excited to watch the city's falcons in action.

Work to Win

Work to Win uses gamification to encourage users to do their chores and homework.

Pothole Association

Pothole Association lets users upload photos of potholes to help the city to fix damaged roads.
