Two Truths & a Lie

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create a two truths and a lie game!

In this project, you will state two facts about yourself and one lie, and we have to guess which one is which!

Say Block

Code your sprite to say block your first truth for 2 seconds.
  • infoI need a hint

Code your sprite to say block your second truth for 2 seconds.

Don't forget to change the time that it shows if your truth is long!

  • infoI need a hint

Code your sprite to say your lie for 2 seconds.
  • infoI need a hint

Put A., B., or C. in front of your 2 truths and 1 lie for 2 seconds.

Test your code. Does it say the three facts with enough time to read each option?

Save your project
Ask Block

Code your game to ask anyone who plays your game which option (A, B, or C) do they think is the lie?
Answer Block

Code your game so that when green flag clicked if the player guesses your lie correctly, then your sprite says “You are correct!” , else your sprite says “You are wrong”

Save your project
Testing

Test your truths first. Does the game tell you that you were wrong?

Now, try out your lie. Does your game tell you that you win?

Congrats! Project complete!
