Following the Mouse

We're going to start by programming a sprite to follow your mouse.

Click on your sprite, I chose a Griffin, but feel free to use something else.

Make your sprite smaller, I chose 20%.

First, let's grab a go to mouse-pointer block.

But we want our sprite to keep on following our mouse, not just once.
Get a forever block, then place around our go to mouse-pointer block.

Now add a when this sprite clicked block on top.

Test your code!
Does your sprite follow the mouse?
Using our maze

Grab an if/then blocks, and place it inside our forever block.
  • infoI need a hint

Now we want to make sure it so that if the sprite touches the obstacles, it will restart at the beginning.

Snag a conditional touching color block.

Don't forget to change the color to the same color as the obstacles! Click on the paint chip and a new menu will pop up.

Move your sprite back to where you want it to start, then grab a go to x:__ y:__ block and place that inside our if/then block.

One more thing, when you hit an obstacle, our sprite doesn't stop following the mouse. Grab a stop all block and place it under our go to x:__ y:__ block.

Reaching our goal

Now Let's add a prize. First, pick any goal sprite, I chose a planet.

Start by dragging a if/then block and place it in ourforever block.
