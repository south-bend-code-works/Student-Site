Controlling the Boat

You are going to control the boat with your mouse. Add code that tells your boat to point towards the mouse-pointer when the green flag is clicked and move steps.

Crashing!

You'll need two costumes for your boat: one normal costume, and one for when the boat crashes. Check out the "costumes" tab!

Duplicate your boat costume.

Name one costume "normal" and the other "hit".

Click on your 'hit' costume, and choose the 'select' tool to grab bits of the boat and move and rotate them around. Make your boat look as if it's crashed.

Add code to your boat inside the forever loop so that if your sprite touches the color brown, then the boat switches to the 'hit' costume.

Have your boat wait 1 second and move back to the start of your course.

Now if you try to sail through a wooden barrier, you should see that your boat crases and moves back to the start.

You can add a speech bubble to make the sprite say something when it crashes!

Save your project
