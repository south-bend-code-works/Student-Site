How to Add a Backdrop

In this lesson you will:
  1. Change the background of a Scratch project.
Let's get started!

Open a new scratch project

Log into your Scratch account.
  • infoHow to sign in

Go to new backdrop

Click Choose a Backdrop if you would like to add one of the backdrops from the scratch library

Want to design your own backdrop?

Click Paintto make your own.

Not sure what kind of backdrop you want?

Click Surprise!

If none of these are working, click Upload, and you can upload a picture from your computer.

If you chose backdrops you don't like, feel free delete them by clicking the X on the top right corner.

Now, start creating!
