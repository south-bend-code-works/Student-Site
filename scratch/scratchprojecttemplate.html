About Me

In this project, you will create a simple line-following game in Scratch. Guide the cats to safety, and don’t let any of them fall through the gaps!

What you will make

Click and drag with the mouse to draw a line with the pencil. Your objective is to stop the cats falling to their doom by creating a safe path to the exit.

Get Started!

Open the starter project !

Title your project with the name you're going to animate.

Characters

The cat sprite that you can see is the Scratch mascot. Let’s get rid of it, by clicking the x in the corner of the cat icon.

Add characters that represent you to your project.

For example, you could add sprites of your favorite sports, food, games, etc.
  • infoHow to add characters

Add at least 3 sprites that represent you + your favorite things.

Save your project
