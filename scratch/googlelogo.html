Make Your Own Google Logo

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create an animated Google's Logo!

Design a Google logo with a funky twist!

This lesson is provided by Google's CS First.

Let's get started!

Open the starter project, and press Remix !

Log into your Scratch account.
  • infoHow to sign in

Title your project Google Logo.
  • infoHow to add a title
    1. Look at the top of the screen for this text box.



    2. Delete the word "Untitled".

    3. Type in a new name for your project.
Costumes

Click on the "Costumes" tab to select your favorite costumes for each letter

Animations

Use animations that you learned in previous Scratch projects to animate each letter.

Combine different code blocks to get the effects that you want!
  • helpI need a hint
    Use the categories in Scratch to get the effects that you're looking for.

    For example, if you want a letter to change colors, click on the Looks category.



    Remember, start all of your code with an Event.

Save your project
  • infoHow to save your project
    Click "File" in the top-left corner



    Click "Save Now"



    Your project is now saved!
