Paint with Terra

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create a painting game using Terra as a stamp.

Move the mouse to move Terra around the screen. Use the up and down arrows to make her move faster or slower.

This lesson is provided by Google's CS First.

Let's get started!

Open the starter project, and press Remix!

Log into your Scratch account.
  • infoHow to sign in

Title your project.
  • infoHow to add a title
    1. Look at the top of the screen for this text box.



    2. Delete the word "Untitled".

    3. Type in a new name for your project.
Movement
We want Terra to always follow the mouse.

Use the Motion and Control blocks to make Terra always move, but, if on edge, bounce.
