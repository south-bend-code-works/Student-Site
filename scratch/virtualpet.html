Virtual Pet

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create a virtual pet to play with!

Click the toys, food and Octie to play with your pet.

Let's get started!

Open the starter project !

Log into your Scratch account.
  • infoHow to sign in

Title your project.
  • infoHow to add a title
    1. Look at the top of the screen for this text box.



    2. Delete the word "Untitled".

    3. Type in a new name for your project.
Characters

The cat sprite that you can see is the Scratch mascot. Let’s get rid of it, by clicking the x in the corner of the cat icon.

Add your favorite animal to be your pet!
  • infoHow to add characters

Add at least 3 sprites for your pet to play with/eat.

Save your project
  • infoHow to save your project
    Click "File" in the top-left corner



    Click "Save Now"



    Your project is now saved!
Stage Backdrop

Add a backdrop.
  • infoHow to add a backdrop
    At the moment, the stage is white, and looks pretty boring! Let’s add a backdrop to the stage, by clicking the blue icon on the right side of the screen.

    Use the picture below as a reference, if needed.

Animations

Brainstorm what you want to happen when your pet plays with each toy or eats the food.

Use at least 2 different types of Events in your project.

Start with some food. Click on the food sprite you added.

Use the when this sprite clicked event and a play sound block to make a chomp sound when the sprite is clicked.
  • infoI need a hint
    Try using these blocks.

Animate one of your toys to turn 60 degrees when you press a key.

Make your pet say their name when you click on the sprite.

Animate your pet to move to a random place when it's clicked.

Add any other animations you'd like!

Save your project

Congrats! Project complete!
