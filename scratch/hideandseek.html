Hide and Seek

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create a simple hide and seek game in Scratch.

Click the sprite when it appears to gain points.

Let's get started!

Open the starter project !

Log into your Scratch account.
  • infoHow to sign in

Title your project.
  • infoHow to add a title
    1. Look at the top of the screen for this text box.



    2. Delete the word "Untitled".

    3. Type in a new name for your project.
Characters

The cat sprite that you can see is the Scratch mascot. Let’s get rid of it, by clicking the x in the corner of the cat icon.

Add the character you want to play hide and seek with to your project.
  • infoHow to add characters

Save your project
  • infoHow to save your project
    Click "File" in the top-left corner



    Click "Save Now"



    Your project is now saved!
Stage Backdrop

Add a backdrop.
  • infoHow to add a backdrop
    At the moment, the stage is white, and looks pretty boring! Let’s add a backdrop to the stage, by clicking the blue icon on the right side of the screen.

    Use the picture below as a reference, if needed.

Coding!

Code your character to disappear and reappear in a random position throughout the game.

You will need an Event block to trigger your code, a Loop and a wait block, a Motion block to make the sprite move randomly, and two Looks blocks to make it disappear and reappear.
  • infoHint
Keeping Score

Add a variable called score.

Set the score to 0 when the green flag is clicked.

Add 1 to score when the sprite is clicked.

Add a cool sound that plays when you click on your character.

Add a message that appears when you click on the character.

Save your project

Congrats! Project complete!
