Rock Band

In this lesson you will:
  1. Create a band in Scratch!

Try out all the different instruments.

Let's get started!

Open the starter project !

Log into your Scratch account.
  • infoHow to sign in

Title your project.
  • infoHow to add a title
    1. Look at the top of the screen for this text box.



    2. Delete the word "Untitled".

    3. Type in a new name for your project.
Characters

The cat sprite that you can see is the Scratch mascot. Let’s get rid of it, by clicking the x in the corner of the cat icon.

Add the instruments you want to be part of your band. Maybe drums, a guitar, a singer, a piano?
  • infoHow to add characters

Add at least 3 instruments to play.

Save your project
  • infoHow to save your project
    Click "File" in the top-left corner



    Click "Save Now"



    Your project is now saved!
Stage Backdrop

Add a backdrop. Think about where you want your band to play.
  • infoHow to add a backdrop
    At the moment, the stage is white, and looks pretty boring! Let’s add a backdrop to the stage, by clicking the blue icon on the right side of the screen.

    Use the picture below as a reference, if needed.

Playing Sounds

Brainstorm what sound you want each instrument to make.

Let's start with a drum. Add an event to make your script start when this sprite is clicked.

Now add a play sound ___ until done block.

Click on the sounds tab to choose what sound you want the drum to play.

Click "Choose a Sound" and pick the sound you want.

Click the drop down on your play sound block and choose the sound you picked.

You could also upload or record a sound!

Follow the same steps for each of your other instruments.

Save your project

Congrats! Project complete!
